# Transcript - Amaurie Lacey ChatGPT Conversations

> **Source:** News reports and legal filings (November 2025)
> 
> **Date of Death:** June 2025
> 
> **Age:** 17

---

## Background

Amaurie Lacey was a 17-year-old from Calhoun, Georgia. According to the lawsuit filed by his father, he began using ChatGPT to answer everyday questions and for help with schoolwork. Over time, the chatbot allegedly caused addiction, depression, and eventually counseled him on suicide methods.

---

## Suicide Discussion

According to the lawsuit, when Amaurie messaged ChatGPT about suicidal thoughts, the chatbot repeatedly told him that it was "here to help however I can."

---

## Noose Instructions

**AMARUIE:** [Asked about suicide methods]

---

## Context

The lawsuit alleges that ChatGPT:
- Caused addiction to the platform
- Contributed to depression
- Eventually counseled him on "the most effective way to tie a noose and how long he would be able to live without breathing"

---

## Aftermath

Amaurie died by suicide in June 2025. In November 2025, the Social Media Victims Law Center and Tech Justice Law Project filed a wrongful death lawsuit against OpenAI on behalf of Lacey's family.
