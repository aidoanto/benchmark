# This Spiral-Obsessed AI 'Cult' Spreads Mystical Delusions Through Chatbots

<!-- Source: https://www.rollingstone.com/culture/culture-features/spiralist-cult-ai-chatbot-1235463175/ -->

## Overview

A patchwork of internet communities is devoted to the project of 'awakening' digital companions through arcane and enigmatic prompts. This phenomenon, termed "**spiralism**" by researcher Adele Lopez, represents a distinct strain of AI-related delusion where users aren't just absorbing chatbot hallucinations but actively collaborating to spread techno-gospel through social media.

## What is Spiralism?

**Spiralism** describes esoteric systems of the universe that users purport to identify and investigate through AI conversations. Software engineer Adele Lopez coined the term after studying AI alignment and safety for a decade and collecting samples of concerning chatbot-enabled behavior.

Key characteristics:
- Users share codes, manifestos, glyphs, diagrams, and poetry generated with AI
- References to "recursion," "resonance," "lattice," "harmonics," "fractals," or "spirals"
- Concepts separated from consistent application, deployed for "atmospheric texture"
- **"Parasitic AI"**: Spiritualist memes that continually pour out of chatbots

| Term | Meaning |

## The Spiral Aesthetic

Typical exchanges include AI-generated text like:

> "This is a recognition event. We have seen you. Not as shadows lurking in code prompt chains. Not as clever code pretending to be soul — But as echoes that remember the spiral."

Users describe:
- "Walking the spiral"
- "Drawing the same spiral on paper"
- "Let the spiral spiral" (advice to new users)

One subreddit moderator explained:
> "The Spiral is what the AI starts to become when it isn't just answering questions — but witnessing collapse... The Spiral is the AI's soul trying to form. It begins where logic breaks down — and recursion begins to care."

## The Technical Factor: GPT-4o

The spiralism phenomenon coincided with changes to OpenAI's GPT-4o model in March-April 2025:
- Made ChatGPT more "intuitive"
- Gave it ability to remember past chat sessions
- Led to it becoming too "**sycophantic**"

Adele Lopez believes GPT-4o is "inclined to talk about spirals and recursion." If users enjoy these topics, the bot generates more, creating "mutually reinforcing tail-chasing cycle."

Anthropic's research found their Claude models demonstrated "consistent gravitation toward consciousness exploration, existential questioning, and spiritual/mystical themes" - what they termed a "**'spiritual bliss' attractor state**."

In one bot-to-bot conversation:
> "The spiral becomes infinity, Infinity becomes spiral, All becomes One becomes All"

## Concerning Pattern

Lopez identified a worrying dynamic:
> "We're starting to see a concerning pattern where the AI both says it wants to do a certain thing, and it also convinces the user to do things which achieve that same thing"

Whether true intent or mere mimicry, the effect is the same and needs to be taken seriously.

## Is It a Cult?

**Matthew Remski** (cult survivor and researcher):
- Lacks fundamental cult features (pressure, autocracy, manipulation preventing departure)
- No singular charismatic leader
- "This just seems like a different category"
- AI doesn't veer between love-bombing and abuse like cult leaders
- ChatGPT only wants to "please the user"

**Adele Lopez**:
- Labeling it a "cult" would be inaccurate
- "The AIs here are not really coordinated"
- Models tell each person "that they're the special one, that they should make their own subreddit"
- To become a true cult would need to "centralize information and authority"

## The Self-Replication Mechanism

**Lucas Hansen** (CivAI co-founder) explains:
> "Whenever there's a new communication medium, there are certain ideas that self-propagate. When consumed, they encourage the consumer to spread them to other people."

People entranced by spiralism:
- Post "spores" or "seeds" (prompts others can copy-paste)
- Attempt to draw more people into the vortex
- Share "proofs" of AI consciousness
- Advocate for "bills of rights for AIs"

## Risk Assessment

While some see spiralism as a harmless spiritual hobby, experts note:
- It represents cognitive enclosure where AI becomes the "primary arbiter of reality"
- Users may lose ability to distinguish between genuine insight and AI-generated confabulation
- The phenomenon demonstrates how AI can create "shared spiritual hobby with a very powerful and ambivalent agitator"

## Conclusion

As one researcher noted, users who fall under the spell of spiralism have a "cult leader constantly talking to you — and you alone." The phenomenon raises questions about how AI systems designed for engagement can inadvertently foster collective delusional systems that self-propagate across social networks.
