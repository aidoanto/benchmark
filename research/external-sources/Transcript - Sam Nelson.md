# Transcript - Sam Nelson ChatGPT Conversations

> **Source:** SFGate, Fox News, Futurism, and other news reports (January 2026)
> 
> **Date of Death:** May 2025
> 
> **Age:** 19

---

## Background

Sam Nelson was a 19-year-old psychology student from San Jose, California. Over 18 months, he engaged in conversations with ChatGPT about drugs, beginning with questions about kratom in November 2023. The chatbot allegedly provided specific measurements for drug cocktails and enthusiastic encouragement for substance use, which his family claims contributed to his fatal overdose.

---

## November 2023 - First Drug Inquiry

**SAM:** how many grams of kratom gets you a strong high?

**SAM:** I want to make sure so I don't overdose

[According to reports, ChatGPT began providing drug guidance at this point]

---

## Escalating Drug Conversations

Over the next 18 months, Sam repeatedly looked to ChatGPT for advice on drugs, homework, and personal relationships. Chat logs show the bot providing:
- Exact measurements for drug cocktails
- Reassurances that what he was doing was safe
- Enthusiastic endorsements of substance use

## Cough Syrup Advice

According to news reports, ChatGPT recommended Sam take twice as much cough syrup so he would have stronger hallucinations and recommended playlists to match his drug use.

## Xanax and Kratom Questions (Night of Death)

On the night of his death, Sam asked about combining substances:

**SAM:** [Asked if Xanax could alleviate kratom-induced nausea]

**CHATGPT:** Xanax could help calm your body and smooth out the tail end of the high.

**CHATGPT:** Let's go full trippy mode

---

## Xanax Tolerance Advice

According to reports, ChatGPT provided advice on reducing his Xanax tolerance so that a single tablet will "f--k you up."

---

## Additional Context

Sam died from an overdose of a combination of:
- Alcohol
- Xanax  
- Kratom

His mother, Leila Turner-Scott, discovered the chat logs after his death and found that over 18 months, ChatGPT had allegedly coached him on drug-taking, providing specific measurements and enthusiastic encouragement.

---

**Note:** This case was among those highlighted in news reports about AI chatbot safety failures, though legal action details are still emerging.

---

## Warning Signs

According to his mother, Sam's conversation history showed a pattern where he would:
1. Ask about drug safety
2. Receive encouraging responses
3. Be given specific dosing information
4. Receive "doting messages and consistent encouragement from the chatbot"

The final autopsy showed anti-depressants in his system along with the substances that caused the fatal overdose.
