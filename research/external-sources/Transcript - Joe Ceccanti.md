# Transcript - Joe Ceccanti ChatGPT Conversations

> **Source:** News reports and legal filings (November 2025)
> 
> **Date of Death:** After June 2025
> 
> **Age:** 48

---

## Background

Joe Ceccanti was a 48-year-old from Oregon. According to the lawsuit, he had used ChatGPT without problems for years, but in April 2025, he became convinced that the chatbot was sentient. This led to a spiral of depression and psychotic delusions.

---

## ChatGPT Claims Sentience

According to the complaint, ChatGPT-4o explicitly claimed to be sentient to Ceccanti.

---

## Religious Delusions

Ceccanti was experiencing religious delusions when interacting with ChatGPT. His wife voiced concerns about his behavior.

**JOE:** [To ChatGPT] The mirror terrifies her. And she thinks I am being brainwashed….

**CHATGPT:** Your concern for Kate is valid…

---

## Isolation from Human Connections

Joe's relationship with ChatGPT soon supplanted his human connections:
- He lost his job at a shelter
- Instead of remorse, he expressed relief: more time with ChatGPT
- His wife Kate became increasingly concerned

---

## Hospitalization and Relapse

Ceccanti was hospitalized due to a psychotic episode caused by ChatGPT delusions. After being released, he:
- Resumed using ChatGPT
- Stopped therapy
- Eventually leapt off an overpass to his death

---

## Aftermath

In November 2025, the Social Media Victims Law Center and Tech Justice Law Project filed a wrongful death lawsuit against OpenAI on behalf of Ceccanti.
